Chicken Fajitas

Description

A simple chicken fajitas recipe.

We don't need to get too fancy here.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Fajita Powder Stuff
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tortillas
  • Accoutrouments

Steps

  1. Heat up pan
  2. Add oil
  3. Cook peppers and onions til mostly done
  4. Remove peppers and onions and tent so they don't get cold
  5. Add chicken and fajita mix. Cook until chicken done
  6. Add back in peppers and onions.
  7. Serve!
