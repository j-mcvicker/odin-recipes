Beef Teriyaki

Description

A simple beef teriyaki recipe.

We don't need to get too fancy here.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Mirin
  • White Rice
  • Seseme Seeds

Steps

  1. Start cooking your rice
  2. Mix together soy sauce brown sugar and mirin
  3. Heat up the pan to medium high
  4. Add beef to the pan
  5. When beef is almost done, throw in the sauce and toss/cook for a 1-3 minutes
  6. Add seseme seeds
  7. Remove from heat, add to rice, serve
