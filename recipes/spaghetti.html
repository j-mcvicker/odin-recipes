Spaghetti

Description

A simple spghetti recipe.

We don't need to get too fancy here.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Olive Oil
  • White Rice
  • Seseme Seeds

Steps

  1. Heat up stove
  2. Brown ground beef
  3. Add pasta sauce. Stir occasionally
  4. Start boiling water for noddles
  5. Add noodles when water is boiling
  6. Remove noodle after about 10 minutes.
  7. Stir some olive oil in to the cooked noodles
  8. Add sauce and serve!
